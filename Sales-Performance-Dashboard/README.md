# Superstore Sales Performance Analysis Using Power BI
## Project Overview
This Power BI project analyzes sales performance, profitability, and product trends from 2014 to 2017. The objective was to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights through interactive visualizations and key performance indicators (KPIs). The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify high-performing product categories, evaluate regional sales performance, and highlight areas requiring management attention.

## Business Questions
The dashboard was designed to answer the following questions:
1. What was the overall profit margin achieved during the reporting period?
2. What was the total sales revenue generated during the reporting period?
3. How did sales performance change over time?
4. Which specific month-year recorded the highest sales?
5. Which product category generated the highest sales revenue?
6. Which region generated the highest sales revenue?
7. Which product sub-categories generated the highest profit?
8. Which sub-categories recorded losses and require management attention?

## Dataset Used
- <a href="https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/konstantinognev/sample-superstorecsv?resource=download">Superstore Sales Dataset</a>

## Data Preparation
The following steps were performed before developing the dashboard:
- Imported and reviewed the dataset in Power BI.
- Verified and cleaned data types for sales, profit, quantity, and date fields.
- Created DAX measures for Total Sales, Total Orders, Total Profit, and Profit Margin.
- Validated calculations and visual outputs to ensure reporting accuracy.
- 
## Key Findings
- The business generated approximately $2.30 million in total sales revenue during the reporting period.
- The company achieved an overall profit margin of 12.47%.
- Approximately 5,000 orders were processed between the year 2014 and 2017.
- Sales generally increased throughout the reporting period, indicating sustained business growth.
- November 2017 recorded the highest monthly sales, generating approximately $118k.
- Technology was the highest-performing product category by sales revenue.
- The West region generated the highest sales revenue among all regions.
- Copiers generated the highest profit among all product sub-categories.
- Tables, Bookcases, and Supplies recorded losses and negatively impacted overall profitability.

## Recommendations
- Continue investing in and promoting high-performing products such as Copiers, which contribute significantly to overall profitability.
- Leverage the successful sales strategies used in the West region to improve performance in lower-performing regions.
- Expand marketing and sales efforts for the Technology category, which generated the highest sales revenue.
- Also review pricing strategies, discount policies, and operational costs for Tables, Bookcases, and Supplies to improve their profitability.
- Analyze the factors that contributed to the strong performance in November 2017 and apply similar strategies during lower-performing periods.

## Conclusion
The analysis identified the West region as the strongest sales market, while Technology emerged as the leading product category by sales revenue. Copiers delivered the highest profitability among all sub-categories, demonstrating their importance to overall business success. Sales reached their highest level in November 2017, suggesting a strong seasonal effect and highlighting opportunities for targeted marketing and inventory planning during peak periods. While overall business performance was strong, loss-making sub-categories such as Tables, Bookcases, and Supplies require strategic review to improve profitability.
